Quick Tip: How to Open Settings

For most features, you'll need to open your Chromebook settings:

  1. Click the time in the bottom-right corner of your screen
  2. Click the gear icon ⚙️ to open Settings
  3. Click "Accessibility" in the left menu

📖 About This App

Empowering accessibility across UK classrooms.

This tool was built and is maintained by GEG England—a community of passionate educators dedicated to sharing the best of Google for Education across the UK.

Why join a GEG?

Being part of a Google Educator Group isn't just about tech; it's about connection. Whether you are a classroom teacher, a lead, or an admin, joining your local GEG gives you:

  • 🤝
    Direct Support Solutions from peers who "get" the UK curriculum.
  • 🚀
    Early Access Insights into the latest Google Workspace and ChromeOS updates.
  • 💡
    Inspiration Real-world examples of how to reduce workload and boost student outcomes.
